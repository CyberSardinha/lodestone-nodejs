# Free Company

Search and get information about Free Companies.

---

## /freecompany

Get a list of free companies

---

## /freecompany/search

Search for a free company

### GET Parameters

> `name={name}`

> `server={server}`

---

## /freecompany/get/{id}

Get a Free Company

---

## /freecompany/get/{id}/members

Get a list of members within the Free Company
